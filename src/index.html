Where Science Defines What is Possible

G-Summit is not an average science conference. It is a rare concoction of the world’s leading scientists, tech executives and investors.

The mission is bold, yet the method is simple. What happens when the brightest minds in artificial intelligence, materials science and biomedicine come together from around the world to share breakthrough research and discoveries? What happens when the best from academia and industry collaborate? What happens when scientists with the capability to change the world work with investors to commercialize ideas and tech executives to distribute it globally. Amazing things happen.

Luminaries

For an exhilarating 36 hours, 100 invited luminaries will meet in San Francisco to learn, share and collaborate. They represent the best in AI, machine learning, computer vision, artificial skin robotics, nanotechnology and neurology.

Tickets

The G-Summit is an invitation only event with limited openings available for leading scientists, executives, and investors. The standard ticket price is $5,000 and includes access to the full summit and event meals.

For scientists looking to participate, a G-Summit Educator pass is available at a discount rate of $1,250.

Join us on September 27-28 at the Hyatt Regency in San Francisco to explore what happens when the world’s leading scientists, technologists and business leaders convene for both scheduled talks, and unstructured activities where the group decides what to talk about. No rules, no agenda - just enthusiastic interactions amongst great minds.

About

G-Summit is a non-profit initiative produced by GWC, one of the world’s foremost influential technology innovation platforms. GWC operates a global network of 800 executives from leading tech companies and a series of annual GMIC mobile conferences in 9 cities including Beijing, Bangalore and Tel Aviv.
